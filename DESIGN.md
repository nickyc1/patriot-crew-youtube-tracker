# Design system

## Scene

Michael or Mark checks this on a laptop or phone during a bright workday and needs one credible answer quickly: did more YouTube pressure produce more customers who say they first heard about Patriot Crew on YouTube?

## Visual strategy

Light, restrained, utilitarian, and close to Patriot Crew's storefront. The page should feel like a sharp internal operating report with the brand's confidence, not a generic analytics product.

## Color

- Canvas: warm off-white, `oklch(97% 0.008 90)`
- Ink: charcoal, `oklch(21% 0.012 80)`
- Muted ink: `oklch(48% 0.012 80)`
- Rule: `oklch(84% 0.012 80)`
- YouTube survey signal: muted red, `oklch(52% 0.17 28)`
- YouTube spend: dark navy, `oklch(34% 0.08 250)`
- Positive/working signal: Patriot olive, `oklch(49% 0.06 105)`

## Typography

- Headings: condensed uppercase system stack, strong weight, tight tracking
- Body and numbers: Roboto/system sans, tabular numerals for metrics
- Body line length: no more than 70 characters

## Layout

- Full-width masthead with logo and reporting date
- One clear thesis statement
- A single dual-axis weekly chart as the main artifact
- An accessible weekly detail table directly below the chart
- A compact methodology and interpretation section, not nested cards

## Interaction

- Hover/focus reveals the exact weekly values
- Responsive chart and horizontally scrollable table on small screens
- No motion beyond subtle opacity and stroke transitions
