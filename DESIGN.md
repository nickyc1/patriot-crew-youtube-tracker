# Design system

## Scene

Michael or Mark checks this on a laptop or phone during a bright workday and needs one credible answer quickly: did more YouTube pressure produce more customers who say they first heard about Patriot Crew on YouTube?

## Visual strategy

Dark, compact, restrained, and modeled on the RAX Daily Ads Dash. The page should feel like a sharp internal operating report with the brand's confidence, not a generic analytics product.

## Color

- Canvas: warm black, `oklch(15% 0.008 85)`
- Panel: `oklch(20% 0.009 85)`
- Ink: warm white, `oklch(94% 0.008 85)`
- Muted ink: `oklch(66% 0.012 85)`
- Rule: `oklch(31% 0.012 85)`
- YouTube survey signal: muted red, `oklch(52% 0.17 28)`
- YouTube spend: dark navy, `oklch(34% 0.08 250)`
- Positive/working signal: Patriot olive, `oklch(49% 0.06 105)`

## Typography

- One system sans family throughout, compact hierarchy, tabular numerals for metrics
- Body line length: no more than 70 characters

## Layout

- Compact masthead with logo and reporting window
- Six executive metrics visible immediately
- One dual-axis complete-week chart
- Four directly playable featured videos for each reporting month
- Collapsible creator rows with playable June videos
- One short attribution footnote

## Interaction

- Hover/focus reveals the exact weekly values
- Responsive chart and horizontally scrollable table on small screens
- No motion beyond subtle opacity and stroke transitions
