# Patriot Crew YouTube Growth Tracker

## Register

product

## Product purpose

A client-facing weekly scorecard that compares Patriot Crew post-purchase survey responses naming YouTube as the first-touch source with total Google Ads spend delivered on YouTube inventory.

## Users

Michael, Mark, and the Patriot Crew growth team. They need to see whether YouTube is growing new-customer awareness even when last-click platform ROAS understates its contribution.

## Primary decision

Should Patriot Crew continue, improve, or scale YouTube creative and spend based on a repeatable weekly acquisition signal?

## Principles

- Show customer-reported acquisition first.
- Separate observed data from interpretation.
- Never claim correlation proves causation.
- Make week-over-week movement readable in under 30 seconds.
- Keep all customer-level survey data private; publish aggregates only.

## Tone

Direct, grounded, plainspoken, and specific. Avoid platform jargon and overclaiming.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboard card grids
- Patriotic visual clichés, flag textures, stars, or novelty military styling
- Dense attribution reports that bury the customer signal
- Decorative gradients, glass panels, or excessive animation
